🧩 Category 🟡 ILC 🔵 IDC 🎯 Therapeutic Implications
🩺 Example Clinical Presentation 👵 Postmenopausal, strongly ER+; subtle/asymmetric thickening (🩻 often underestimated); later GI/peritoneal symptoms 🫄 🤏 Palpable spiculated mass with clear mammographic correlate; later 🦴 bone pain 🧭 For ILC: obtain breast MRI for extent; anticipate diffuse margins; endocrine-first often reasonable
📊 Prevalence ~10% of breast cancers Most common subtype 📝 ILC underrepresented in trials → avoid one-size-fits-all extrapolation
🔬 Morphology “Single-file” 🧵 non-cohesive cells; hallmark = E-cadherin loss (CDH1) 🚫 Cohesive nests/glands 🧱 🔎 E-cadherin loss aids diagnosis; expect diffuse growth → margin planning matters
🧬 Genomics ↑ CDH1, PTEN↓/AKT↑, TBX3, FOXA1; ↓ GATA3 🧬 More GATA3; rare CDH1 mutation Distinct ER co-factor wiring → different endocrine sensitivity/resistance biology
🌸 Hormone/Proliferation Mostly ER+, HER2–; low Ki-67 🌿; Luminal-A–like Mixed: Luminal A/B, HER2+, TNBC Endocrine-driven strategy in many ILCs
🧭 Metastatic Pattern GI 🍽️, peritoneum 🫄, ovaries 🥚 ↑ Bone 🦴, lung 🌬️, liver 🧫 common Tailor staging/surveillance; in ILC consider GI/peritoneal evaluation when symptomatic
💉 Chemo Response (Neoadjuvant) Lower pCR (~3–5%) ⚠️; less downstaging; higher positive margins/mastectomy Higher pCR (~14%) ✅; more downstaging & BCS 🎯 Use chemotherapy selectively in ILC; prefer biomarker-guided selection
🧪 Oncotype DX High-risk RS rare (~1.5%); many intermediate 🤔 Up to ~20% high RS Intermediate RS less informative in ILC → integrate clinicopathologic context
🧿 HER2 & Trastuzumab Classic ILC 3–5%; mixed/non-classic up to ~9–10% ➕; trastuzumab effective 💉 ~15–20%; anti-HER2 standard 🧪 Don’t assume HER2– in ILC; test and treat — benefit ≈ IDC
🌡️ Endocrine Therapy Aromatase inhibitors (e.g., letrozole) > tamoxifen 🟢 AI benefit clearest in Luminal-B; less in Luminal-A Postmenopausal ILC: prefer AI; monitor TAM resistance mechanisms
📉 Prognosis Often lower grade & slow-growing ⏳ but risk of late recurrence; occult/diffuse disease & atypical GI/peritoneal mets Greater chemo-sensitivity may allow earlier downstaging 💥 🗺️ ILC: emphasize accurate extent (MRI), margin strategy, endocrine durability, and vigilance for late relapse
