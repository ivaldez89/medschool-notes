ILC vs IDC — Differences & Therapeutic Implications

🟡 ILC 🔵 IDC
🧩 Category 🟡 ILC 🔵 IDC 🎯 Therapeutic Implications
📊 Prevalence ~10% of breast cancers Most common subtype Often underrepresented in trials → avoid one-size-fits-all.
🔬 Morphology Single-file non-cohesive cells; hallmark = E-cadherin loss Cohesive nests/glands; variable E-cadherin loss = diagnostic marker; diffuse growth pattern.
🧬 Genomics ↑ CDH1, PTEN loss/AKT, TBX3, FOXA1; ↓ GATA3 More GATA3; rare CDH1 loss Different ER cofactors → therapy response differs.
🌸 Hormone/Proliferation Mostly ER+, HER2–; low Ki-67; luminal A–like Heterogeneous (luminal A/B, HER2+, TNBC) Endocrine-leaning strategy in ILC.
🧭 Spread GI, peritoneum, ovaries Bone, lung, liver Surveillance tailored to pattern.
💉 Chemo Response Lower pCR (3–5%); less downstaging Higher pCR (~14%); better BCS Chemo selectively in ILC; biomarker guided.
🧪 Oncotype DX Rare high RS (~1.5%); many intermediate Up to 20% high RS Intermediate RS less informative in ILC.
🧿 HER2 & Trastuzumab 3–10%; trastuzumab effective 15–20%; trastuzumab standard Test all ILC for HER2; benefit ≈ IDC.
🌡️ Endocrine Therapy AI (letrozole) > tamoxifen AI better mainly in luminal B Prefer AI in postmeno ILC.
🧠 Buzzwords “Single-file”, “E-cadherin−”, “GI/peritoneal mets”, “AI>TAM” “Cohesive glands”, “GATA3”, “Chemo-responsive” Use for memory anchors.
🩺 Example Presentation Postmeno, ER+ subtle thickening; underestimated on imaging; later peritoneal spread Palpable spiculated mass; bone pain if metastatic ILC: consider MRI for extent; endocrine-first.
Source: Barroso-Sousa R, Metzger-Filho O. Differences between invasive lobular and invasive ductal carcinoma of the breast: results and therapeutic implications. PMCID: PMC4952020, PMID: 27482285.
