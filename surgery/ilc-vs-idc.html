🧩 Category 🟡 ILC 🔵 IDC 🎯 Therapeutic Implications
🩺 Example Clinical Presentation 👵 Postmeno, ER+ mass; subtle/asymmetric thickening (often underestimated on 🩻); later GI/peritoneal spread 🫄 🤏 Palpable spiculated lump; clear imaging correlate; later 🦴 bone pain 🧭 ILC: order MRI for extent; endocrine-first in many cases
📊 Prevalence ~10% of BCs Most common subtype 📝 ILC underrepresented in trials → don’t assume IDC data applies
🔬 Morphology “Single-file” 🧵 cells; hallmark = E-cadherin loss 🚫 Cohesive nests/glands 🧱 Loss of cohesion → diffuse margins; plan wider resections
🧬 Genomics ↑ CDH1, PTEN loss/AKT↑, TBX3, FOXA1; ↓ GATA3 🧬 More GATA3; rare CDH1 loss Distinct ER co-factor wiring → different endocrine sensitivity
🌸 Hormone/Proliferation Mostly ER+, HER2–; low Ki-67 🌿; Luminal-A–like Mixed: Luminal A/B, HER2+, TNBC Endocrine-driven in ILC
🧭 Spread GI 🍽️, peritoneum 🫄, ovaries 🥚 Bone 🦴, lung 🌬️, liver 🧫 Tailor staging/surveillance beyond classic bone/liver mets
💉 Chemo Response Low pCR (~3–5%) ⚠️; less downstaging Higher pCR (~14%) ✅; more BCS possible Use chemo selectively in ILC; prefer biomarker-guided approach
🧪 Oncotype DX Rare high RS (~1.5%); many intermediate 🤔 Up to 20% high RS Intermediate less predictive in ILC → integrate pathology/clinicals
🧿 HER2 & Trastuzumab Classic ILC 3–5%; mixed up to ~10% ➕; trastuzumab effective 💉 15–20%; anti-HER2 standard Always test HER2 in ILC; benefit ≈ IDC
🌡️ Endocrine Therapy AIs (letrozole) > tamoxifen 🟢 AI benefit clearest in Luminal-B Postmeno ILC: AI first-line
📉 Prognosis Slower growing ⏳ but **late** recurrences; atypical GI/peritoneal mets More chemo-sensitive; earlier downstaging 💥 ILC: rely on MRI for extent, plan margins, watch late relapse
🧠 Buzzwords “Single-file” 🧵 • “E-cadherin−” 🚫 • “GI mets” 🫄 • “AI>TAM” 💊 “Cohesive glands” 🧱 • “GATA3” 🧬 • “Chemo-responsive” 💥 Use for rapid recall 🔑
